About Calm Waters

Calm Waters is a peaceful fishing game designed for moments of relaxation. Cast your line, catch fish, and earn gold at your own pace. No timers, no pressure—just you and the water. Created as a school project for Web Technologies.

How To Play

Click to cast your line and catch fish for gold. Use your gold to buy rod upgrades or unlock new biomes. Check your Stats to track progress. That's it.
